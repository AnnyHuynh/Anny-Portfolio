About Me


I am a full-stack web developer with an accounting/financing background. I started out as a self-taught web developer by learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, and building websites. The more I learn, the deeper I get hooked into it, and I get really engaged in the programing landscape. I was trying to learn, practice, and consume as much information as I can. I strive to learn new technologies because I'm fascinated with how technologies can connect people together, and help people work more efficiently. I'm also passionate in digging deeper into developing and designing websites which brings me to get enrolled in Web Development Program at the University Of Washington. I love using my creativities and skills to design websites. I also love the mental challenge that programming presents, and I appreciate how an idea is transformed into reality. The more I code, the more exciting I get to grind deeper into programming and to be a better developer.
