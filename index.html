About Me


My name is Anny Huynh. I was born in DaNang, Viet Nam to a business family with strong values of open mindedness, hard work, loyalty, patriotism and adaptation.

I'm an animal lover and I'm upset with dogs, especially with my Beagle dog named Kiwi. I also love traveling and trying good food around the world.

I want to get my foot in the door in the growing tech industry as a JavaScript developer, creating functional code for web apps.

I'm currently an Cost Accountant with over 5 years of accounting experiences. I want to transition into programing because I’m very fascinated with what coding can do to develop the world’s technology and change people’s lives. I’m always passionate at solving problems, designing, and creating. With coding, I love designing webpages, and use Javascript to program the behaviors of my webpages.

I graduated in 2014 with a BA degree in Accounting at the Central Washington University, and I also got a Full Stack Web Developer Certification at the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp in April 2019.

I have experienced with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, ReactJS, Handlebars, Bootstrap, JSON, AJAX request, API, MySQL, Firebase, MongoDB, Terminal, Heroku and Git.
